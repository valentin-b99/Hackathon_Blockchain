Welcome Valentin !

What is the CREP?

The CREP, also called "Cryptocurrency Epitech", is as its name suggests a cryptocurrency.

A cryptocurrency is a non-physical currency that can be used on a decentralized computer network.

CREP is the currency of the IONIS group created for group students.

What is it for?

The CREP makes it possible to buy services such as "live coding" (Epitech), activities organized by the BDE.

How to earn CREPs?

Winning CREP students by obtaining degrees at the various modules.

Indeed, these are evolutionary. That is, your rating is good, you earn credit.

In addition, it is also possible to obtain services for your school.

The creation of CREP...

The CREP was created on 19 December 2018 by two Epitech students during a Hackaton Blockchain challenge.

The goal of this challenge was to create a cryptocurrency for IONIS schools.
